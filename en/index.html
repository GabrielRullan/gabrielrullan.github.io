What is a Data Partner?

A Data Partner goes beyond the traditional role of a service provider. As your Data Partner, I act as an extension of your team, deeply invested in your success.

Is this not consulting?

Consulting firms often try to capture clients, they are not interested in relationships unless they are long and expensive projects. What I want is to create synergies and only necessary projects.

I have limited data, I don't want anything complicated

We will create a customized solution, with the level of complexity adapted to your company.

What is the name of what I am selling?

I don't have a solution to sell you, I work with your problems and find your solution.

Why not just hire internally, if data is so important?

It is not worth it to have a whole data team in-house, but to begin your data journey you'd need to hire experts, and then keep them with you.

Can you give me an example?

Think of it like you needing a Data Office. I will come, get the requirements, build you office, show you how to mantain it and use it and then just stay in touch if you need more "big" things. The operations and day to day will be all yours, no extra dependencies.
